Our Capabilities

Civil Mine & Quarry (CMQ) Geotechnics is a specialist consultancy that provides technical services to the civil, mining, quarrying, and land development sectors. The company is managed by professionals with sound academic qualifications and professional experience. The specialist services of the group consist of various expertise centres including:

  • Civil and Mine & Quarry Geotechnical Engineering
  • Geophysical Investigations
  • Pavement Engineering
  • Geomechanics
  • Research & Development
  • Total Solutions (Design, Deliver & Certify)

Our Approach & Philosophy

Our services are underpinned by training and applied research in areas that enable the formulation of economical, practical risk-free and technically sound solutions for our Clients. Our underpinning philosophy is to put clients first and foremost, accordingly our team seeks to work collaboratively with clients to develop fit for purpose solutions.

Civil Geotechnical Engineering

We provide an array of geotechnical services consisting of:

  • Geotechnical investigations and consultancy
  • Engineering geology for civil engineering projects
  • Geophysical investigations for civil engineering projects
  • Geotechnical instrumentation and monitoring
  • Geo-hazard mapping, assessment of cavities and slope stability

We can deliver geotechnical solutions for small subdivisions to large facilities and linear developments over hundreds of kilometres for roads, rails, and pipelines. Recognising the risks associated with lack of site investigation data, our staff combine geophysical investigation techniques for ground investigation with traditional techniques. This enables technically robust and cost-effective investigations and designs to be carried out. Our approach enables us to deliver economical solutions for developments in soft ground and high-risk ground such as karst land, collapsing soils, or swelling reactive clays.

Mine and Quarry Geotechnics

Our team has an established experience base for project development and for operating mines that covers the following areas:

  • Risk assessment, mitigation and management
  • Advanced geotechnical modelling
  • Advanced mine design and extraction sequencing, to optimise slope design
  • Design and development of slope support regimes for optimum excavation
  • Open pit and underground void interaction, i.e., mining through old workings
  • Data collection, interpretation and analysis
  • Hydro-geological evaluation and modelling, including recommendations for pit dewatering
  • Geotechnical Studies: Scoping, PFS, FS
  • Strategic design of slope monitoring systems and back analysis of data for pit optimisation purposes
  • Forensic review/analysis of pit slope failures
  • Mine and Quarry Closure and Rehabilitation considerations with respect to slope/void stability
  • Review/formulation of ground control management plans
  • Mining methods and operation planning criteria
  • Workforce training and competency evaluation
  • Mentoring and skills transfer/training

Geophysics

Our team is experienced in geophysical investigation methods such as:

  • Seismic Refraction
  • Multi-channel Analysis of Surface Waves
  • Ground Penetrating Radar
  • Electromagnetics
  • Electrical Resistivity
  • Gravity and similar techniques

These techniques have been developed to ensure adequate geotechnical information can be obtained rapidly, economically, and non-invasively with minimum ground disturbance to reduce the geotechnical risks associated with projects.
